发表于 2017-03-17 |

nth-child是针对一个列表的选项进行针对性的操作，其系列可供在我们各种情况下使用

  • :first-child
  • :first-of-type
  • :last-of-type
  • :only-of-type
  • :only-child
  • :nth-child(n)
  • :nth-last-child(n)
  • :nth-of-type(n)
  • :nth-last-of-type(n)
  • :nth-last-of-type(n)
